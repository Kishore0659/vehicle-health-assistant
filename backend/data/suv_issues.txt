Problem: SUV 4WD system not engaging
Cause: Faulty transfer case, damaged 4WD actuator, broken shift motor, low transfer case fluid
Solution: Check transfer case fluid, replace actuator, repair shift motor, rebuild transfer case
Severity: High
Cost: INR 3000 to INR 40000

Problem: SUV engine overheating under load
Cause: Insufficient cooling capacity, clogged radiator, failing water pump, thermostat stuck closed
Solution: Flush and refill coolant, clean radiator, replace water pump and thermostat
Severity: High
Cost: INR 2000 to INR 30000

Problem: SUV rear suspension sagging
Cause: Worn coil springs, failed air suspension, broken leaf springs, worn shock absorbers
Solution: Replace coil/leaf springs, repair air suspension compressor, replace shocks
Severity: Moderate
Cost: INR 5000 to INR 35000

Problem: SUV excessive body roll while cornering
Cause: Worn sway bar links or bushings, failed shock absorbers, soft suspension tuning
Solution: Replace sway bar links and bushings, replace shock absorbers
Severity: Moderate
Cost: INR 2000 to INR 12000
