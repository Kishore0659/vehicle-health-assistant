Problem: Car engine not starting
Cause: Dead battery, faulty starter motor, bad ignition switch, empty fuel tank, or security immobilizer activated
Solution: Jump-start or replace battery, replace starter motor, check ignition switch, refuel, reset immobilizer
Severity: Moderate
Cost: INR 500 to INR 8000

Problem: Car engine overheating
Cause: Low coolant level, faulty thermostat, broken water pump, clogged radiator, or leaking head gasket
Solution: Top up coolant, replace thermostat, replace water pump, flush radiator, fix head gasket
Severity: High
Cost: INR 1000 to INR 25000

Problem: Car brake making squealing noise
Cause: Worn brake pads, glazed rotors, dust or debris on brakes, low-quality brake pads
Solution: Replace brake pads, resurface or replace rotors, clean brake assembly
Severity: High
Cost: INR 1500 to INR 8000

Problem: Car AC not cooling properly
Cause: Low refrigerant, faulty compressor, clogged cabin air filter, condenser blockage
Solution: Recharge refrigerant, replace compressor, change cabin filter, clean condenser
Severity: Low
Cost: INR 500 to INR 15000

Problem: Car making grinding noise when turning
Cause: Worn CV joints, low power steering fluid, damaged wheel bearing, or worn tie rod ends
Solution: Replace CV axle/joints, top up power steering fluid, replace wheel bearing, replace tie rod ends
Severity: High
Cost: INR 2000 to INR 12000

Problem: Car vibrating while driving
Cause: Unbalanced or worn tyres, bent rim, loose wheel bolts, worn shock absorbers
Solution: Balance and rotate tyres, replace rim, tighten wheel bolts, replace shock absorbers
Severity: Moderate
Cost: INR 500 to INR 10000

Problem: Car engine oil light on dashboard
Cause: Low oil level, oil pressure sensor failure, oil pump failure, or severe oil leak
Solution: Check and top up engine oil, replace sensor, repair oil pump, fix leaks
Severity: High
Cost: INR 300 to INR 20000

Problem: Car check engine light on
Cause: Faulty O2 sensor, loose fuel cap, catalytic converter failure, MAF sensor issue
Solution: Run OBD-II diagnostic scan, tighten fuel cap, replace faulty sensor or converter
Severity: Moderate
Cost: INR 500 to INR 30000

Problem: Car transmission slipping or jerking
Cause: Low transmission fluid, worn clutch (manual), solenoid failure, or damaged transmission
Solution: Check and top up transmission fluid, replace clutch, repair solenoid, rebuild transmission
Severity: High
Cost: INR 2000 to INR 60000

Problem: Car battery draining quickly
Cause: Parasitic drain, alternator not charging, old battery, leaving lights on
Solution: Check for electrical drain, test and replace alternator, replace battery
Severity: Moderate
Cost: INR 1000 to INR 8000

Problem: Car fuel efficiency has dropped significantly
Cause: Dirty air filter, faulty oxygen sensor, low tyre pressure, fuel injector clogging
Solution: Replace air filter, replace O2 sensor, inflate tyres, clean or replace injectors
Severity: Low
Cost: INR 300 to INR 5000

Problem: Car windows fogging up from inside
Cause: Heater core leak, high moisture inside cabin, faulty AC dehumidifier
Solution: Check heater core, use AC to dehumidify, check cabin ventilation
Severity: Low
Cost: INR 200 to INR 12000

Problem: Car suspension making clunking noise
Cause: Worn ball joints, broken sway bar links, worn strut mounts, loose exhaust components
Solution: Replace ball joints, replace sway bar links and mounts, tighten exhaust
Severity: High
Cost: INR 2000 to INR 15000

Problem: Car steering wheel shaking at high speed
Cause: Wheel imbalance, worn tie rod ends, damaged CV joint, loose steering components
Solution: Balance wheels, replace tie rod ends, replace CV joint, check steering rack
Severity: Moderate
Cost: INR 500 to INR 10000

Problem: Car producing white smoke from exhaust
Cause: Coolant burning in engine, blown head gasket, cracked engine block, condensation (normal when cold)
Solution: Check coolant level, inspect head gasket, pressure test cooling system
Severity: High
Cost: INR 5000 to INR 50000

Problem: Car door lock not working
Cause: Faulty actuator, broken linkage, blown fuse, dead remote battery
Solution: Replace door lock actuator, fix linkage, check fuse, replace remote battery
Severity: Low
Cost: INR 300 to INR 4000

Problem: Car burning smell from brakes
Cause: Stuck brake caliper, new brake pads bedding in, overheating brakes from downhill driving
Solution: Check caliper for sticking, allow new pads to bed in, use engine braking on hills
Severity: High
Cost: INR 1500 to INR 10000

Problem: Car hard to start in cold weather
Cause: Thickened engine oil, weak battery, fuel line issues, glow plug failure (diesel)
Solution: Use recommended grade engine oil, replace battery, replace glow plugs
Severity: Moderate
Cost: INR 500 to INR 6000
