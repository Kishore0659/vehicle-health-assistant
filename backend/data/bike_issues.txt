Problem: Bike engine not starting
Cause: Dead battery, faulty spark plug, clogged fuel line, or empty fuel tank
Solution: Check battery voltage (should be 12V+), replace spark plug, clean fuel line, refuel
Severity: Moderate
Cost: INR 200 to INR 2500

Problem: Bike engine overheating
Cause: Low coolant (for liquid-cooled), clogged air filter, lean fuel mixture, or continuous high-speed riding
Solution: Let engine cool, check coolant level, clean or replace air filter, check carburetor jetting
Severity: High
Cost: INR 500 to INR 5000

Problem: Bike brake not working properly
Cause: Worn brake pads, air in brake lines, low brake fluid, or warped disc rotor
Solution: Replace brake pads, bleed brake lines, top up brake fluid, resurface or replace disc rotor
Severity: High
Cost: INR 300 to INR 3000

Problem: Bike producing black smoke from exhaust
Cause: Rich fuel mixture, clogged air filter, worn piston rings, or oil burning
Solution: Adjust carburetor or fuel injector, replace air filter, check piston rings and valve seals
Severity: Moderate
Cost: INR 500 to INR 8000

Problem: Bike chain slipping or making noise
Cause: Loose or stretched chain, worn sprockets, insufficient lubrication
Solution: Adjust chain tension, lubricate chain, replace chain and sprockets if worn
Severity: Moderate
Cost: INR 300 to INR 3500

Problem: Bike vibrating excessively at high speed
Cause: Unbalanced wheels, loose wheel bearings, bent rim, or worn tires
Solution: Balance wheels, replace wheel bearings, check and replace rim, replace tires
Severity: Moderate
Cost: INR 500 to INR 4000

Problem: Bike not picking up speed (poor acceleration)
Cause: Clogged carburetor jets, faulty throttle position sensor, worn clutch plates, choked air filter
Solution: Clean carburetor, check TPS, replace clutch plates, replace air filter
Severity: Moderate
Cost: INR 400 to INR 6000

Problem: Bike headlight not working
Cause: Blown fuse, faulty bulb, broken wiring, or bad headlight switch
Solution: Check and replace fuse, replace bulb, inspect wiring harness, replace switch
Severity: Low
Cost: INR 100 to INR 1500

Problem: Bike fuel leakage
Cause: Cracked fuel pipe, loose fuel cap, faulty petcock, or damaged carburetor float
Solution: Replace fuel pipe, tighten fuel cap, replace petcock, repair or replace carburetor
Severity: High
Cost: INR 200 to INR 3000

Problem: Bike clutch slipping
Cause: Worn clutch plates, weak clutch springs, improper clutch adjustment
Solution: Adjust clutch cable free play, replace clutch plates and springs
Severity: Moderate
Cost: INR 800 to INR 4000

Problem: Bike making knocking noise from engine
Cause: Low engine oil, worn piston, loose connecting rod, carbon buildup
Solution: Check and top up engine oil, inspect piston and connecting rod, decarbonize engine
Severity: High
Cost: INR 1000 to INR 15000

Problem: Bike tyres losing air frequently
Cause: Puncture, valve stem leak, damaged rim seal
Solution: Repair or replace tube/tyre, replace valve stem, check rim for damage
Severity: Low
Cost: INR 100 to INR 1500

Problem: Bike indicator lights not working
Cause: Blown bulb, faulty relay, broken switch, blown fuse
Solution: Replace bulb, replace indicator relay, check switches and fuses
Severity: Low
Cost: INR 50 to INR 800

Problem: Bike engine oil leaking
Cause: Worn gaskets, loose drain plug, cracked engine casing, damaged oil seal
Solution: Replace gaskets, tighten drain plug, repair casing, replace oil seal
Severity: High
Cost: INR 500 to INR 6000

Problem: Bike starting but stalling immediately
Cause: Idle speed too low, clogged pilot jet, faulty idle air control valve
Solution: Adjust idle speed screw, clean pilot jet, check IAC valve
Severity: Moderate
Cost: INR 300 to INR 2500
